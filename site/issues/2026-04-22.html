Business Data Fragmentation Costs

SAAS opportunity · Professional Services
Problem

The business owner is struggling with data fragmentation across multiple SaaS vendors, making it difficult to query and analyze their own business data. This is resulting in wasted time and resources, with engineers having to build custom pipelines to answer simple questions. The owner is essentially 'renting' their own data back from these vendors, incurring a hidden tax on every business decision.

Solution

An AI-powered data integration and analytics platform could help solve this problem by providing a unified view of the business's data, allowing for easy querying and analysis across different systems. This could automate the process of building custom pipelines and reduce the need for IT tickets. The platform could also provide real-time insights and recommendations to inform business decisions.

Monetisation

The solution could be offered as a SaaS subscription-based model, with tiered pricing based on the number of integrations and data volume.

Trend

The need for data integration and analytics is a growing trend, as more businesses adopt multiple SaaS vendors and generate increasing amounts of data.
